Welcome

you can modify the URL with the following

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"/calc"

for calculator, you must give the calculator val1 & val2 & operation values

example: /calc?val1=10&val2=100&operation=*

note that "+" works in the format of "%2b"

other viable operations is "/", "*", "-", and of course "%2b"

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"/img"

provides you with one of two random images of me snowboarding, you can generate the other picture by refreshing the page

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"/csv"

prints sample.csv document to Json format

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

"rps" (POST)

You can play Rock Paper Scissors against the computer by filling out the form below

by refreshing the URL you will get a Json formatted result in GET
